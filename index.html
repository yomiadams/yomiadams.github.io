About Me

I hail from Warri in Delta State, Nigeria. My academic journey began with a Bachelor of Science in Estate Management. Post-graduation, my career has been a diverse tapestry of roles, including UX design, business analysis, data analysis, graphic design, and business process improvements across various organizations. I am eagerly looking forward to earning an MBA with a concentration in Business Analytics and Information Systems. My ambition is to harness the power of data, design thinking, and process improvement to contribute significantly to achieving organizational goals and objectives.
